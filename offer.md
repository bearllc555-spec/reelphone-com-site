## Product definition

ReelPhone is a dedicated Android-based content and posting system for brands and agencies that need more short-form content without building a full internal creative team.

A customer sends a product photo or media plus a short prompt. ReelPhone generates UGC-style video concepts and scripts, creates short-form videos, pushes them to a dedicated ReelPhone device, posts them to the customer's social accounts, and reports back with status and links.

This is a **hardware + software + service offer**, not just software.

## Core promise

> Send a product photo. ReelPhone turns it into UGC-style social videos and posts them for you.

## Use cases

### Primary use case

A brand sends a product image and prompt such as: *"Make UGC-style short videos for this hoodie."*

ReelPhone then:

1. Generates concepts and scripts
2. Produces short-form videos
3. Pushes them to the ReelPhone device
4. Posts them to connected social accounts
5. Sends back status and links

### Secondary use case

Agencies use ReelPhone to fulfill recurring short-form content packages for multiple clients without adding as much manual production overhead.
